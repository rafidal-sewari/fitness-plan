Protein batch
Chicken & beef
1Season 4 lbs chicken breast with salt, garlic powder, paprika. Bake at 400°F for 22–25 min. Cool, slice, portion into 7 containers (6 oz each).
2Brown 2.5 lbs lean ground beef in a skillet, drain fat, season with salt and pepper. Portion into 7 containers (6 oz each).
Potato batch
Covers breakfast, lunch & dinner
1Halve a 5 lb bag of baby potatoes. Toss in olive oil spray, salt, garlic powder, and pepper.
2Roast at 425°F for 25–30 min on 2 sheet pans, flipping halfway. Portion 150g per container. Reheat 2 min in microwave.
Veggies
Broccoli & greens
1Steam or microwave 1 large bag broccoli. Portion 1.5 cups into each lunch container.
2Buy pre-washed mixed greens — grab and serve at dinner, no prep needed.
Breakfast & snack
Zero to minimal prep
1Keep eggs and egg whites in the fridge. Cook fresh each morning (~5 min). Microwave pre-prepped potatoes while eggs cook.
2Buy Greek yogurt in a bulk tub and a box of protein bars. Keep berries in the freezer — thaw overnight or 30 sec in microwave.
